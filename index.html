Workshop on Quantum Information and Quantum Dynamics

Workshop Details and Registration

The Workshop on Quantum Information and Quantum Dynamics aims to gather leading experts and researchers in the field to discuss recent advancements and explore collaborative opportunities. Participants will engage in insightful discussions and presentations, fostering knowledge exchange and partnership in quantum science. This workshop is funded by Seed Funding for Collaboration and Partnership Projects, IRCC, IIT Bombay and organized by the Department of Physics, IIT Bombay.

Date: 29 October 2024

Venue: Room No. 23, Victor Menezes Convention Centre, IIT Bombay
